# Opensource Coffee Shop Playbook ☕

An open-source, practical repository for planning, launching, and operating a **coffee shop or coffee cart**.

This project aims to be the “missing public playbook” for independent coffee businesses, with templates, checklists, and examples that anyone can copy, adapt, and improve.

> **Disclaimer:** This is not legal, tax, or financial advice. Regulations and best practices vary by country, region, and city. Always confirm details with local professionals and authorities.

---

## Who this is for

- People planning a **coffee cart** or **tiny kiosk**
- People opening a **small independent coffee shop**
- Operators who want structure for **systems and SOPs**
- Community members who want to **share what worked** in their region

---

## What’s inside

- **Docs** (`/docs`): Guides for concept, market research, business planning, operations, menu, branding, legal, etc.
- **Templates** (`/templates`): Copy-and-adapt outlines, checklists, and CSVs.
- **Examples** (`/examples`): Sample setups (e.g. low-budget cart vs. small shop).

---

## Quick start

1. Read `docs/01-concept-and-vision.md` and write down your concept.
2. Use `docs/02-market-research.md` to sanity-check your idea.
3. Fill in `templates/business-plan-outline.md` for your specific location.
4. Build your first rough numbers in `templates/financial-model-structure.csv`.
5. Start drafting your **menu and operations** using:
   - `templates/daily-operations-checklist.md`
   - `templates/barista-training-checklist.md`
   - `templates/supplier-list-template.csv`
